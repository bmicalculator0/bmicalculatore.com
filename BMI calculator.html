Diet Plan

Based on your BMI category, here's a simple diet plan. Consult a doctor for personalized advice.

  • Underweight (BMI < 18.5): Focus on calorie-dense foods like nuts, avocados, and whole grains. Aim for 3 meals + snacks daily.
  • Normal (BMI 18.5-24.9): Balanced diet with fruits, veggies, lean proteins, and whole grains. Maintain portion control.
  • Overweight (BMI 25-29.9): Reduce processed foods and sugars. Emphasize veggies, lean meats, and exercise. Calorie deficit of 500/day.
  • Obese (BMI ≥ 30): Low-calorie, high-fiber diet. Consult a nutritionist for meal plans and weight loss goals.

About BMI

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's a simple tool for assessing health risks, but not perfect—muscle mass can affect results. Categories: Underweight (<18.5), Normal (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), Obese (≥30).
